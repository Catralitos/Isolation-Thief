There’s a pandemic ravaging the world. The number of infected people continues to grow. People are forced to leave work and take refuge in their homes. But you’re a thief! There’s no remote working for you. And if you’re planning to still make ends meet, you’re gonna have to keep doing your job. Even though it just became a whole lot harder…

Isolation Thief is a first-person stealth game developed in Unity as part of Instituto Superior Técnico's Game Design Course.

Game Design: Bernardo Pinto 

Level, Quest, and Systems Design: Carlos Marques

Programming: Bernardo Pinto, Carlos Marques, Daniel Correia and Lidia Gonçalves

House Model: Lidia Gonçalves
